Stay Indoors. Stay Healthy





According to a new study, COVID-19 stay-at-home orders have had a negative impact on health behaviors, including sleep quality, physical activity, weight maintenance, and consumption of sweets.

Excercising Your Brain Muscle

Your brain has the ability to learn and grow as you age — a process called brain plasticity — but for it to do so, you have to train it on a regular basis.

Fulfilling Your Body Needs

Since exercise is known to have an impact on the functioning of the immune system, it is important to make sure that is still a priority even when staying indoors.

Exploring Your Interests

Health experts recommend keeping up with hobbies at home as a distraction from stressful and negative thoughts. Creative outlets such as music, art, and journaling can be therapeutic. Anyone can get started with it from instructional videos and tutorials!
